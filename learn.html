The Chatoreyz Story

Founded by Chef Atul Gour, Chatoreyz is more than just a restaurant - it's a culinary journey through the vibrant flavors of India. Our passion for authentic recipes, fresh ingredients, and innovative cooking techniques has made us a favorite among food lovers in Indore. At Chatoreyz, we believe that great food brings people together. Whether you're joining us for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a special celebration, we promise an unforgettable dining experience that will keep you coming back for more.
